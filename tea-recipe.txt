Ingredients:

Tea Leaves
Milk
Sugar
Water


Method:

Add water to a pan and put it on the stove.
add some milk to it (water:milk = 1:1.5)
add tea leaves
let it heat so that the concoction boils for a couple of times. Regulate the burner to achieve this.
When color of the tea is suitable to your taste, switch off the stove and transfer contents to a cup.
Add sugar according to taste.
